Welcome to my homepage!

I am an associate professor and assistant director of the [Institute of Mathematical Sciences](https://ims.shanghaitech.edu.cn) at [ShanghaiTech University](https://www.shanghaitech.edu.cn), where I have been a faculty member since 2019. Prior to that, I held postdoctoral positions at Bonn, Mainz, Bath, and Hannover. 

I received my PhD in Mathematics from Stanford University in 2009, under the supervision of Professor Jun Li. I received my BSc in Mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2004, under the supervision of Professor Sen Hu.

My primary research interests lie in algebraic geometry, with a particular focus on the interaction between moduli spaces of sheaves and holomorphic symplectic manifolds. More specifically, my interests include:
- Moduli spaces of sheaves and complexes on Calabi-Yau manifolds
- Holomorphic symplectic manifolds and their degenerations
- Derived categories, stability conditions, and wall-crossing
